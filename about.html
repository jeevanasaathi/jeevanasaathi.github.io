Our Story

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts.

  • We met at PES University, section 1A.

    August 7, 2017

    Jeevana was (and still is) the teacher's favorite, while I was always getting in trouble thanks to the people around me. Eventually, I moved to her bench to escape the chaos, only to end up arguing with her instead (for the record, I was right, she was wrong!). Despite the bickering, we somehow became friends.

  • We became Best Friends.

    Somewhere along the next 4 years...

    We’d sneak out of class for endless Taboo battles, cram for tests at the last minute, and share bags of chips between lectures. Our study sessions always turned into laughter-filled hangouts, making every day in college feel like an adventure. Because as Shahrukh Khan said, pyaar... dosti hai—and that’s exactly what made those days unforgettable.

  • We graduated together, side by side.

    May 12, 2021

    From unforgettable nights filled with music that still gives us chills to challenging days we conquered together, we both found our way to incredible careers. Our journey was just beginning, and we knew the best was yet to come.

  • Saahil moved to the U.S., only for Jeevana to follow soon after.

    August 16, 2022 – November 25, 2022

    Pursuing my Master’s degree at NYU in New York kept me busy as I adapted to my new environment, but distance didn’t change a thing between us. Just a couple of months later, Jeevana was right there, making sure we stayed as connected as ever—I guess she missed me too much!

  • Different Roads, Same Adventures

    May 16, 2024

    After months on opposite sides of a new country, we still found ways to stay close, meeting up whenever we could. When graduation day arrived, Jeevana flew in to celebrate with me, the Geet to my Aditya. This achievement felt like a shared journey—one we couldn’t have reached without each other’s encouragement along the way.

    November 11, 2024
